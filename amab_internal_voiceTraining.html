Voice Training

Unfortunately even if you choose to pursue HRT, it will not effect your voice. Though vocal feminization surgery is a further option that can be looked to, we will discuss that further later.


There is many youtube videos online, but there is a voice feminization training program through TransCare BC that you can find information about here. In order to apply you will need to fill out this application form and email it to: transcareteam@phsa.ca
