Binding

There are different kinds of binding, but safety is an important thing to remember. Here is a safety resource from Transcare BC.

A binder is a piece of compression clothing that flattens your chest down. Binders come in a half-length (crop top style) or full-length (tank top style). The style you choose depends on your own preference and comfort.

Kinetic tape is also an option. This type of medical tape is for your skin and can be used to bind your chest. Do not use other tapes, such as duct tape or plastic wrap.If you have sensitive skin, you may experience irritation from binding tapes.
